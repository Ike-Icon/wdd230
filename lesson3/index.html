Suncity Chamber of Commerce for Health

purpose

The major purpose of the Suncity Chamber of Commerce for Health is to assist people who require medical assistance and even to establish a foundation for persons who require surgery or cancer treatment. The Suncity Chamber of Commerce for Health is an organization comprised of health professionals as well as business owners, firms, and industries.
