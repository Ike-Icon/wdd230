Overview

Site Description

With its corporate office in Bethesda, Maryland, The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel business that develops and operates specialized lodgings close to temples for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the globe. The owner-operated business provides specific services and lodgings that are tailored to the requirements of the temple workers, visitors, and their families. Anyone willing to follow the company's rules and policies is eligible to use the hotel's amenities and services.

Site Name

The website is referred to by the moniker TISBethesda.com, where TIS stands for The Temple Inn & Suites. Located in Bethesda, it is a hotel chain.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate visitors that they should choose our hotels over others because of the quality of our services and the accessible and welcoming settings we provide. Additionally, we have highly trained personnel who are enthusiastic about their work. The website project's overarching objective is to publicize and disseminate details about the specialized services that this hotel chain provides in order to cater to the needs of temple visitors who travel to the temple to serve there or who attend events like sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips. The website should embody the spirit of the Church and be built around a temple-focused motif, as well as the idea of being a welcoming haven for all.

Site Map

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
