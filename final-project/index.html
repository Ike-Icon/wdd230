Hotel Overview

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain with its corporate office in Bethesda, Maryland. We develops and operates specialty hotels close to temples for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world.

The owner-operated business provides specific services and lodgings that are tailored to the requirements of the temple workers, visitors, and their families. Anyone willing to follow the company's rules and policies is eligible to use the hotel's amenities and services. The main objective is to raise awareness of and provide details on the specialized services that this hotel chain provides in order to cater to the requirements of temple visitors who travel to serve in the temple or who attend activities like sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth outings.
